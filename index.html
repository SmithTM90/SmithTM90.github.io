As a Front-End Web Developer I get to create effective, and memorable designs and functionality for others to enjoy. I try to see the world through other peoples eyes, which rewards me with a sense of empathy and understanding, and allows me to incorporate the human experience in my work. I strive to be adaptable and always look for the next creative solution.I am a full stack web developer, working with up to date technologies to create sustainable and professional products. Learning to utilize new skills as I progress as a developer is super exciting! Efficiency is my game.
