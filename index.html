About

I am a full stack web developer, working with up to date technologies to create sustainable and professional products. I am a firm believer that actions speak louder than words. I gained most of my knowledge through General Assembly in Seattle, the immersive course that I attended was top notch and I learned SO MUCH! I really enjoy developing because the possibilities are so vast, I can do so many things to create innovative solutions and bring them to light. Learning to utilize new skills as I progress as a developer is super exciting! Efficiency is my game.

I became a full stack developer in 2016, I began the journey back in 2015 when I moved to Seattle, and it has been a wild ride. Web development came as a major career and life change for me when I moved back to Washington (My home state) from my then-home in Missouri. When life gives you lemons, turn them into a new set of skills! When I'm not working on my development skills, I enjoy video games, music, board games, and general merriment with a small group of friends.
